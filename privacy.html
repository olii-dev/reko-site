Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At Reko, we believe your data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data.

Reko is designed to function entirely locally on your device. Every movie you add to your watchlist, every rating you give, and every preference you set is stored securely on your own iPhone using iOS's native storage systems (UserDefaults and SwiftData).

We do not have servers that store user accounts, and we do not track your usage behavior.

2. Third-Party Services

Reko uses The Movie Database (TMDB) API to fetch information about movies and TV shows (images, descriptions, cast lists, etc.).

  • When you search for a title or view details, the app sends a request to TMDB servers to retrieve that specific information.
  • We do not send any personally identifiable information (PII) to TMDB.
  • Use of TMDB services is subject to TMDB's Privacy Policy.

3. Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics, Firebase, or Mixpanel) inside the Reko app to track user behavior.
