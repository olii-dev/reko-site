Frequently Asked Questions

Is Reko free?
Yes! Reko is completely free to download and use. No catches either, your data is safe and never shared.
How do recommendations work?
Reko analyses your favourite movies and shows to find connections: same actors, same directors, similar genres, and collaborative filtering data. The more you use it, the better it gets!
Which devices are supported?
Reko is available on iOS devices running iOS 17 or later.
Do you need an account?
No account required! Your data is saved locally on your device.
Where does the data come from?
All movie and TV show data comes from TMDB (The Movie Database), one of the most comprehensive entertainment databases available.
